GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

Having a good health helps save life.

Accessible health reduces mortality rate.

At its current pace, achieving the Sustainable Development Goals, including Goal No. 3 on Good Health and Well-being, will take immense effort and resources. According to Philippine Statistics Authority data, only one target for SDG 3—Universal Health Coverage—is on track.
