About Me

I am Ifan Jon T. Maniquiz from Cavite, with a passion for programming and technology. I have experience in C++, Python, and web development using HTML and CSS. My interest in robotics began in junior high school at General Emilio Aguinaldo National High School, where I participated in robotics programs during Grades 7 and 8.

Although I did not focus on programming in that project, I later contributed to the development of AWLMEWS (Automated Water Level Monitoring and Early Warning System) during Grade 12. This system utilized Arduino and IoT technology for flood monitoring and alerts.

Currently, I am continuing my studies at FEU Institute of Technology, further developing my skills and knowledge in programming and technology.
